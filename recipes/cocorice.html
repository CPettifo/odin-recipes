Coconut Rice

Description

This is a restaurant style creamy coconut rice which is very simple and cheap to make. The sugar in the recipe adds a complexity to the taste. The washing and soaking of the rice results in a fluffy texture for the finished coconut rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Rice
  • 125mL water
  • 400mL Coconut Milk
  • Tablespoon of sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Wash and drain the rice three times, or until the water is clear
  2. Soak the rice for 15 minutes
  3. While the rice is soaking, mix the water, sugar and salt
  4. Put the rice, water mixture and coconut milk into a shallow pan uncovered at a medium heat
  5. Once the water is boiling stir once, lower the heat and cover the pan
  6. After 15 minutes the rice should be fluffy and cooked
  7. Serve in your choice of bowl
