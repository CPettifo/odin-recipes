Steak

Description

This is a non-vegetarian steak

Ingredients

  • 1 steak
  • 2 tbs olive oil

Steps

  1. Allow steak to get to room temperature
  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan
  3. Once the oil is hot, add the steak to the pan
  4. Turn the steak once after 5 minutes
  5. After five more minutes remove the steak from the pan and allow to rest for two minutes
  6. Serve
