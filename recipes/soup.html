Pumpkin Soup

Description

This is a restaurant style pumpkin soup with stock

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions finely chopped
  • 1kg of pumpkin
  • 700mL veggie stock
  • 150mL double cream

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan and cook the onions for 5 minutes
  2. Add the pumpkin and cook for 8-10 minutes
  3. Pour 700mL of vegetabl stock and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Pour the double cream into the soup and bring to a boil. Blend into a puree and serve.
